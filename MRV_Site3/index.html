The Vision

Artist.
Storyteller.
Visionary.

Born in Fairbanks, Alaska and raised in a military family across multiple states and Germany, Mike Ray's journey was shaped by the discipline and determination of his father — an Army Sergeant who passed from ALS at 46, leaving a legacy of resilience.

An award-winning filmmaker and multi-hyphenate, Mike began directing to channel his creative energy, mastering the art of delivering big-budget aesthetics on independent budgets. His work spans documentary, episodic, feature, and broadcast television.

With 15+ years of voice-over experience, credits as a radio host on PowerTalk, and the role of narrator for Carl Weber's The Man in 3B, Mike brings a distinct voice — literally — to every project.

With over 15 years of professional voice-over experience, Mike Ray brings authority, warmth, and distinctive presence to every recording. From audiobook narration to radio hosting, his voice is as compelling as his camera work.
