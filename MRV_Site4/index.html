About

Mike Ray

Born in Fairbanks, Alaska and raised across America and Germany in a military family, Mike Ray carries the restless determination of a man shaped by constant motion. When his father — an Army sergeant — died of ALS at 46, that legacy became a mandate: tell every story like it might be your last.

An award-winning filmmaker, Mike built his reputation on making low-budget productions look like big-budget films. His festival-touring documentary The Helen Lindsey Story and episodic feature The Productive Lie stand as proof of that philosophy.

Beyond the lens, Mike is a voice artist with 15+ years of experience, narrator of Carl Weber's The Man in 3B, comedian, radio host on PowerTalk, and creator of the board game Journey to the Motherland. Four projects are currently in development: two sitcoms, a feature film, and a web-isode series.

Voice Over & Narration

The Voice
Behind the Story

With over 15 years of professional voice-over experience, Mike Ray brings an unmatched combination of depth, authority, and warmth to every recording. His most notable narration work includes Carl Weber's bestselling The Man in 3B — bringing the characters to life with an intimacy that only a true performer can deliver.

Available for audiobooks, commercials, corporate narration, trailers, and character voiceover. Fully equipped home studio.
