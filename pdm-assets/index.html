✅ Logo DOs & DON'Ts

DOs

  • Use on navy or white backgrounds
  • Maintain clear space equal to logo height
  • Use full-color version as primary
  • Scale proportionally only
  • Use white version on dark backgrounds

DON'Ts

  • Don't stretch or distort
  • Don't change brand colors
  • Don't add drop shadows
  • Don't place on busy backgrounds
  • Don't use below 32px width
