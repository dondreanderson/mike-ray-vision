About Mike

From Alaska to the World Stage

Award-Winning Filmmaker & Entertainer

Born in Fairbanks, Alaska, Mike Ray grew up in a military family, living across the United States and Germany. His father, an Army sergeant who passed from ALS at 46, left a lasting legacy of determination and resilience.

As an award-winning filmmaker, Mike started directing to stay creative and busy, focusing on low-budget productions that achieve a big-budget look. His recent documentary The Helen Lindsey Story and episodic feature The Productive Lie are currently on the festival circuit.

With over 15 years of voice-over experience, Mike brings characters to life with his distinctive voice. He's also the host of PowerTalk radio show and creator of the board game Journey To The Motherland.

Mike narrated Carl Weber's The Man in 3B and continues to expand his presence in film, television, and digital media.
