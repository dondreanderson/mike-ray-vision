Professional CAD Drafting & Design

At Premier Design & Media, we provide comprehensive CAD (Computer-Aided Design) services throughout Georgia. Whether you're planning a residential renovation, commercial development, or industrial facility upgrade, our experienced CAD professionals deliver precision drawings and 3D models that bring your vision to life.

With over 15 years of experience, we've drafted everything from small home additions to large warehouse facilities. Our CAD services ensure accuracy, compliance, and seamless collaboration with architects, engineers, and contractors.

Our CAD Capabilities

  • Residential Drafting

    Home additions, renovations, new construction, kitchen and bathroom remodels, deck and patio designs

  • Commercial Projects

    Office buildouts, retail spaces, restaurant layouts, tenant improvements, accessibility upgrades

  • Industrial Facilities

    Warehouse layouts, manufacturing floor plans, equipment placement, material flow optimization

  • 3D Modeling

    Detailed 3D visualizations, realistic renderings, virtual walkthroughs, client presentations

  • As-Built Documentation

    Accurate existing condition drawings, renovation planning, facility management documentation

Our Process

1

Consultation & Site Assessment

We begin with a thorough consultation to understand your project requirements, timeline, and budget. Our team conducts a detailed site assessment to gather necessary measurements and information.

2

Concept Development

Based on your requirements, we develop initial design concepts and layouts. We collaborate closely with you to refine the design until it perfectly matches your vision.

3

Detailed Drafting

Our CAD professionals create comprehensive construction drawings including floor plans, elevations, sections, and detail sheets. All drawings comply with local building codes and regulations.

4

3D Visualization

We develop detailed 3D models and renderings that help you visualize the finished project. This aids in decision-making and ensures everyone is aligned on the final design.

5

Delivery & Support

Final drawings are delivered in multiple formats (PDF, DWG, DXF) suitable for permitting, bidding, and construction. We provide ongoing support throughout the construction phase.
