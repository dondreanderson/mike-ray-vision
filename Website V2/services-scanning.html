Advanced 3D Laser Scanning Technology

Premier Design & Media utilizes state-of-the-art 3D laser scanning technology to capture precise spatial measurements of any environment. Our high-resolution scanners create detailed point cloud data that serves as the foundation for accurate as-built documentation, renovation planning, and digital twin creation.

Whether you need to document a warehouse, historic building, manufacturing facility, or residential property, our laser scanning services provide unparalleled accuracy and completeness. We've scanned spaces ranging from single rooms to multi-story industrial complexes throughout Georgia.

Our Scanning Capabilities

  • Large Space Scanning

    Warehouses, factories, distribution centers, and commercial buildings - we handle spaces of any size

  • Residential Scanning

    Complete home documentation, room additions, renovation planning, and existing condition surveys

  • Point Cloud Processing

    Professional processing and registration of scan data, noise filtering, and data optimization

  • BIM Integration

    Convert point cloud data to Revit models, AutoCAD drawings, and other CAD formats

  • Virtual Tours

    Create immersive 360° virtual tours and interactive walkthroughs from scan data

Technology & Accuracy

We use professional-grade laser scanners with accuracy up to ±2mm at 10 meters. Our equipment captures millions of measurement points per second, ensuring complete coverage and exceptional detail. The resulting point clouds provide a comprehensive digital record of your space.

Accuracy: ±2mm @ 10m
Scan Speed: 1M pts/sec
Range: 0.1m to 130m
Full Color HDR Imaging

Deliverables

We provide scan data in multiple formats to suit your project needs:

  • Raw Point Cloud Data

    E57, PTS, PTX, RCP formats for maximum flexibility and compatibility

  • CAD Drawings

    Accurate 2D floor plans, elevations, and sections derived from scan data

  • 3D Models

    Revit BIM models, SketchUp models, and other 3D formats

  • Virtual Presentations

    Interactive 3D viewers and video flythroughs for stakeholder presentations
