About Premier Design & Media

15+ years of precision, innovation, and excellence

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Premier Design & Media has grown from a small CAD drafting service to a comprehensive design and media production company serving clients throughout Georgia. Our journey has been driven by a commitment to precision, innovation, and client satisfaction.

Today, we combine traditional CAD expertise with cutting-edge 3D laser scanning technology and professional media production capabilities. This unique combination allows us to serve a diverse clientele, from homeowners planning renovations to large industrial facilities requiring comprehensive documentation.

What sets us apart is our people. Our team of experienced professionals brings decades of combined expertise and a passion for delivering exceptional results on every project, no matter the size.
